About the Project

The Universal Serial Monitor is a robust, lightweight, and cross-platform desktop application designed to facilitate the monitoring, logging, and analysis of serial data from embedded devices (ESP32, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, STM32). It was developed to support air quality monitoring research at the Federal University of Cear√° (UFC).
